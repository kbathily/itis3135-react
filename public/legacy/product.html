Featured Product: StudySprint Planner

StudySprint Planner is a lightweight digital planner designed to help students map weekly goals, block distraction time, and keep assignments visible in one place.

Why It Stands Out

  • Simple weekly dashboard for courses and deadlines
  • Quick task entry with priority markers
  • Clear progress summaries for each class
  • Mobile-friendly layout for checking tasks between classes

Ideal Users

This concept is aimed at college students who want a focused planning tool without the clutter of a full project-management platform.

It is especially useful for students balancing multiple assignments, lab work, and weekly study goals.
